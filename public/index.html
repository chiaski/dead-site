About Writing Experiments Résumé

Chia (they/them) is a Computing & the Arts senior at Yale, from Manila. They are a creative technologist creating radical systems for creators and communities in pursuit of making creation ubiquituous, as the internet age had given them.

This website is a showcase of process (design, dev, & writing) and the half-baked. I'm shy, but I would love to meet you.

Most presently, I'm interested in the internet (its histories and cultures), applying worlding to the web, music journalism, and making terrible audio recordings.

I'm spending summer in Seattle with Spotify and Kumu, plus archiving lore and fictions.

I'm thinking about full-time opportunities starting 2022. Let's talk?
