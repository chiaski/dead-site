Context

In the middle of the rapidly growing Philippine technology ecosystem, there are little publication or news efforts that detail the work of the ecosystem in-depth. I sought to fill in this gap in a digestible format, building Technology.ph to simplify the way people discover the ecosystem—and making it significantly more engaging to the masses.
